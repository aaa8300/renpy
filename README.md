# Case File: The Stinging Swindler

**Hack Club Campfire Miami (2026)**  
1st Place Overall • Best Art • GitHub Special Recognition  

---

## Overview

*Case File: The Stinging Swindler* is a mystery visual novel developed in 24 hours for Hack Club’s Campfire Miami game jam.

The game follows an investigator examining a series of disappearances connected to a beachside underwater cave. Players progress through dialogue, analyze clues, and uncover the truth behind the case.

- **Engine:** Ren’Py  
- **Genre:** Mystery / Visual Novel  
- **Development Time:** 24 hours  

---

## About Campfire

Campfire is a synchronized global game jam organized by Hack Club, where high school students in 200+ cities build games from scratch over a single weekend.

Campfire Miami was the in-person event where our team collaborated, developed the project, and presented it to judges at the end of the 24-hour sprint.

After winning locally, our project advanced to Hack Club’s global online jam, **Ember**, where selected teams refined and showcased their games internationally.

## How We Built It

We began with a core mystery concept and structured the narrative before development began. Our workflow:

1. Outlined the story structure and key plot points  
2. Wrote dialogue and branching choices  
3. Designed character sprites, backgrounds, and UI assets  
4. Composed and integrated original music  
5. Implemented all components in Ren’Py  

Due to the 24-hour time constraint, we focused on delivering a complete and polished experience rather than expanding scope beyond what was realistic.

GitHub was used for version control and project organization throughout development.
